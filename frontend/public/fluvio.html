01 — 03 · The Protocol

Real streaming. Not a simulation.

Fluvio is Initia's native money streaming protocol — the first protocol where "real-time" actually means real-time. Instead of sending 1,000 INIT in a single lump transaction, you deposit into a Fluvio vault and define a flow rate. The contract streams 0.000012 INIT to the recipient every 100 milliseconds — the exact interval of Initia's block time.

The recipient's claimable balance grows every single block. They can withdraw at any moment — mid-stream, without cancelling. The sender can cancel at any time, and unstreamed INIT returns to their vault instantly.

This is money streaming as infrastructure, not as a UI trick.

02 — 03 · The Advantage

Superfluid fakes it. We don't.

Superfluid on Ethereum is the market leader in money streaming, with over $500M TVL. But it has a dirty secret: it doesn't actually stream. Superfluid calculates your "real-time balance" client-side using JavaScript. The Ethereum blockchain sees nothing between 12-second blocks. The streaming is an illusion rendered in your browser.

On Fluvio, the Move function timestamp::now_milliseconds() returns a value that actually changes every 100ms — because Initia produces a block every 100ms. The state is real. The blockchain knows about every millisecond.

03 — 03 · The Architecture

Three contracts. One ecosystem primitive.

Fluvio is three Move contracts deployed on Initia testnet at address init17g5nnyjfkhnjg4w2m82m9st6lxdhuw62zjgsmd.

stream_vault.move handles escrow. stream_core.move is the heart. stream_registry.move is a public oracle any contract on Initia can query.

Use Cases

Real-time Payroll

Replace monthly salary runs with continuous streams. Employees and contractors receive INIT into their .init wallet every millisecond they work. No more waiting for payday — your balance grows as you work.

Subscriptions

Stream subscription fees instead of charging monthly lump sums. Subscribers can cancel the stream at any moment and the unstreamed portion returns to their vault instantly. No chargebacks. No disputes.

Infrastructure Rental

Pay for servers, APIs, storage, and compute by the millisecond. The moment you stop your stream, payment stops. No monthly invoices. No overpaying for idle resources.

Protocol Grants

Stream grants to developers building in the Initia ecosystem. Instead of large milestone payouts, grant funds flow continuously — aligned with ongoing contribution.

Gaming Rewards

Every second a player spends in a game, INIT flows into their .init wallet. True play-to-earn — not a promise, not a token unlock schedule, but a live stream of value.

Cross-chain Streaming

Deposit USDC from Ethereum via Interwoven Bridge. It arrives as INIT in your Fluvio vault. Start streaming immediately to any .init address on Initia.

How It Works

01

Connect Your .init Wallet

Open Fluvio and connect with Keplr. Initia's .init username system means your streaming identity is human-readable. Every stream shows real names, not hex strings.

02

Deposit INIT to Your Vault

Deposit INIT from your connected wallet into your personal Fluvio vault. Coming from another chain? Use the Interwoven Bridge directly in the app.

03

Create a Stream

Click "New Stream." Enter the recipient's .init username, the total amount, and the duration. Approve once with a session key and the stream begins.

04

Watch Money Flow

Your dashboard shows every incoming and outgoing stream with a live counter ticking every 100ms. Recipients can withdraw at any time without cancelling.
